# Managing Homesickness During Break

Feeling homesick during the holidays and breaks hurts even more than normally missing home. And because there are no classes, staying away from home on break seems more boring than ever. Hopefully, these tips will help you feel better, as well as pass the time in ways you've never thought of before.

## 1. Reach Out
Even when you're not missing home, it's incredibly important to reach out to your loved ones to remind them you're there and remind yourself that you have people in your corner. Losing that physical proximity can make you feel more distant, both physically and emotionally, which increases that feeling of loneliness. Call your family, those relatives you haven't spoken to since the last family gathering, those high school friends you swore you'd keep in touch with in college. We have so many platforms that help people connect virtually, so let's use them!

## 2. Entertainment
Go do something fun! See what's new on Netflix; go watch that show that always shows up on your TikTok For You page. Pick up that old book you have lying around that you said you would finish. There are so many things you can do, it's just a matter of what is going to capture your attention.

## 3. Explore Your Campus
It's so easy to hide your head behind your textbooks and problem sets that sometimes you forget to look up and see what's around you right now. Wherever you may be, look around and explore a little! Go into buildings you've walked by but never entered. Maybe you'll discover a new coffee place that has the best cakes, or find a new study spot that no one else knows exists. Invite your friends, too.

## 4. Self-Care
Do a face mask. Fix your nails. Put some music in the background as you fold your clothes. Get a workout in to let those endorphins out. Or maybe you'd rather do some relaxing yoga and meditate afterward. Self-care is different for everyone, so do whatever it is that makes you relax. If you enjoy cooking, make that meal that reminds you of home. Another activity I like to do is journaling, which helps you get your thoughts down as well as find gratitude in your life.

## 5. Make New Plans
It sucks not to be with family now, but it won't suck as much if you know you're going to be able to go home in the future. If going home isn't doable, maybe you can invite your friends from other colleges to come visit when they're on break! Or make plans with your friends who are staying on campus, too. As long as you have something to look forward to, that is all that matters.
