# Williams Dining Services

Williams Dining Services operates several dining halls and mobile-ordering venues across campus. Contact: 413.597.2121 | Droppers House, 15 Park Street, Williamstown, MA 01267. Menus and nutrition information are available through the NutriSlice platform.

## Dining Halls

### Driscoll
Open daily (Sunday through Saturday).
- Breakfast: 7:30–10:30 am
- Lunch: 11:30 am–2:00 pm
- Dinner: 5:00–8:00 pm

### Mission Park
- Sunday: Brunch 11:30 am–1:30 pm, Dinner 4:30–6:30 pm
- Monday–Thursday: Dinner 5:00–8:00 pm

### Whitmans'
- Monday–Friday: Breakfast 7:30–10:30 am, Lunch 11:30 am–2:00 pm, Dinner 5:00–9:00 pm, Late Night 9:30 pm–1:00 am
- Saturday–Sunday: Brunch 11:00 am–2:00 pm, Dinner 5:00–9:00 pm, Late Night 9:30 pm–1:00 am

Whitmans' is the only dining hall offering late-night service, running until 1:00 am every day of the week.

## Mobile Ordering Only Venues

Students access mobile ordering through the GET App using their Williams credentials.

### 82 Grill
- Monday–Friday: Lunch 11:00 am–3:00 pm
- Wednesday–Sunday: Dinner 4:00–8:00 pm

### Lee Snack Bar
- Monday–Friday: Breakfast 7:30–10:30 am, Lunch 11:30 am–2:00 pm, Mid-Day 2:00–5:00 pm, Dinner 5:30–8:00 pm

### Fresh 'n Go
- Monday–Friday: Lunch 11:00 am–2:00 pm

### Eco Cafe
- Monday–Friday: Breakfast 7:30–10:30 am, Lunch 11:00 am–2:00 pm
- Operates with mobile pre-ordering; orders due by 5 pm for next-day pickup.
